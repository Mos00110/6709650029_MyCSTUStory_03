CSTU Story

The first time I entered Thammasat University was during the Open House in 2022 and 2023. It made me realize that this university is more than just a place for education. On the 5th and 6th of August, I met the seniors and friends in the Faculty of Science, Computer Science Department, for the first time. Everyone welcomed me warmly, they are so friendly and king and the activities were fun The official beginning of the university was on 13th of August, I have learned and experienced so much in just one month. I've learned about programing, attended a concert, cycled around the university, played board game, enjoyed the Ong Sa Mhu Kra Ta with friends and seniors, sang karaoke, and even played badminton with seniors. I'm confident that there is still much more to do during my time at Thammasat, as this is only the beginning.
